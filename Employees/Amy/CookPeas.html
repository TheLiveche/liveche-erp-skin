How to Blanch Green Beans

Bring a large pot of water to boil over high heat

Add salt to the water

Add the vegetables to the pot in small batches so that the water continues to boil.

Take them out after 2 minutes

Add them to the bowl of ice water

Strain the peas

Place in bain marie
